Welcome to my homepage! Here you will find information about myself and my research.
I am currently a Research Fellow at the Clinical Operations Research Unit (CORU) at University College London (UCL). Here I work on problems relating to machine learning applied to operational research problems in healthcare. I received my PhD from the University of Liverpool in February 2023 under the supervision of Professor Karl Tuyls and Professor Rahul Savani.
